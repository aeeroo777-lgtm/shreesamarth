What sets us apart

Patient Safety First

Every procedure follows the highest sterilisation and safety protocols. Our ICU is equipped for round-the-clock critical monitoring.

Expert Specialists

Our panel includes Dr. Bhushan and Dr. Ashwin Charkha, with decades of combined expertise in spine, laparoscopic, and trauma surgery.

Affordable Pricing

We believe quality healthcare should be accessible. Our transparent pricing ensures no hidden costs, with insurance assistance available.

In-House Pharmacy

Our 24/7 pharmacy stocks all essential medications, ensuring seamless post-operative and ongoing care without leaving the premises.

Modern Diagnostics

State-of-the-art laboratory services and imaging facilities enable fast, accurate diagnoses for quicker treatment decisions.

Compassionate Care

We treat every patient like family. Our nursing staff is trained to provide emotional support alongside clinical excellence throughout recovery.
